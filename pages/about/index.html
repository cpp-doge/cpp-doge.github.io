About This Blog

This is a blog about mostly C++ programming, mostly for newbies.

If you are already advanced then you are generally not the target audience.

Simple, approachable and easy to understand is the goal here.

  • Reduced cognitive load and reduced barriers to entry are paramount here
  • Deals frequently & frivolously in hyperbolic incendiary claims
  • Wantonly discards pedantry in favor of punchiness, humor, sarcasm and staying power in the mind
  • Calously ignores details in favor of simplicity and ease of understanding for the layman

As my Calculus professor once said:

We will make blanket statements and tell small lies to aid in understanding. We will try to clear things up as soon as possible.
