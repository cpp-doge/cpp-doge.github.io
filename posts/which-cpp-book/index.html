Why Do I Need A Book

There is still no other way to learn about many important topics in C++ other than from books.

YouTube tutorials are mostly bad and wrong. My experience shows that newbies struggle with YouTube tutorials more than they help.

Learn C++ is a good free online textbook, but it’s missing some important information you can still only get from other proper books.

How Do I Choose A Book

You should probably choose a book mostly based on your experience level and preferred learning style.

  • A book for a beginner may not be good for an experienced programmer.
  • A book for an experienced programmer may not be good for a beginner.

How Do I Know It’s Right For Me

Jungle Book, Bigs & Noses and other sellers usually allow you to see the first few pages of a book including the table of contents and the preface.

The preface should usually explain things like how difficult the book is, what the book aims to teach and who the book is intended for.
