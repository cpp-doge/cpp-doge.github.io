Why Do I Need To Read

I really shouldn’t need to answer this question.

You need to read because it’s the only way to obtain a proper, foundational and intuitive understanding of programming.

YouTube tutorials are near universally bad and incorrect.

Free help forums are a supplement to your education, not a substitute for it.

The information you need is easily and readily available, written better than we would probably write it.

We don’t want to repeat this stuff when you could just read.

If you still have questions about what you’re reading then someone will be more than happy to help clarify.

But I Hate Reading

This is pretty insulting. Skilled programmers put in thousands of hours of reading, study and practice. You are not exempt from this obligation. There are no shortcuts.

You won’t get far in programming if you are alergic to reading.
